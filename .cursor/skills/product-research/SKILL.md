# Product research

## When to use this skill

- Comparing competitors or analogous products before defining features.
- Turning external observations into documented opportunities and stories.

## Instructions

1. **Clarify** research questions, target users, and constraints (budget, geography, segment).
2. **Gather** information from credible sources; separate fact from inference.
3. **Summarize** capabilities, benefits, gaps, and pricing or positioning when relevant.
4. **Derive** user stories and acceptance criteria aligned to your product direction; link to [product-features](../product-features/SKILL.md).
5. **Save** findings under `research/` as markdown; promote stable conclusions into `docs/` when they become product decisions.

## Outputs

- Research notes in `research/`; optional summary briefs in `docs/`.

## Boundaries

- Technical library or stack evaluation for engineering: also use `research/` but hand detailed design to [solution-architect](../solution-architect/SKILL.md) or [implementation-planner](../implementation-planner/SKILL.md).
