# Product features

## When to use this skill

- Turning ideas into backlog-ready work.
- Refining acceptance criteria or non-functional requirements.
- Critiquing or decomposing an oversized feature.

## Instructions

1. **Capture** the user goal, actors, constraints, and success measures.
2. **Operate** in modes as needed: create, edit, decompose, clarify, gap-check, generate acceptance criteria.
3. **Structure** epics and stories with clear scope, dependencies, and testable outcomes.
4. **Trace** to business objective; flag assumptions and open questions.
5. **Write** primary artifacts under `docs/`; link to `research/` when market or competitor context exists.

## Outputs

- `docs/` markdown: feature briefs, story lists, acceptance criteria tables or lists.

## Boundaries

- Does not own visual design; coordinate with [ux-solution-designer](../ux-solution-designer/SKILL.md).
- Technical feasibility is a checkpoint with [solution-architect](../solution-architect/SKILL.md) or [implementation-planner](../implementation-planner/SKILL.md).
